Vessels Features

Our vessels are built to the highest standards and are brand new, containing all the latest technology and safety equipment.


Powering

Twin Johnson 225 4 stroke engines comply with latest environmental standards. Quiet & smooth running to produce effortless & exhilarating performance.

Tubes

Heavy duty hypalon tubes incorporate heavy duty fendering and full life lines. Compartmentalised to provide the ultimate in safety.

Hull

With over 10,000 vessels produced the Humber range is known as a world leader. The combination of heavy build, fine lines and build quality makes these hulls superb sea going vessels.

Vessels have been drop tested from 5.5m, undertaken full stability calculations and are built to SOLAS standards as well has having Cat B rating.

Seating

Comfortable seats are made out of robust aluminium to provide high level of support with comfortable padded seating. There is ample provision for under seat storage and access.

Bow Platform

Access to and from the vessel is via a tailor made v-berth. Passengers can get on and off the boat in complete safety using integral steps and a boarding platform.

Safety First

At London RIB Voyages your safety is obviously of paramount importance.

Life Jackets
We supply children and adults of all ages and sizes with regularly tested life jackets. These will be issued to you at the kiosk where the kiosk manager will help you fit the life jacket and ensure it is adjusted properly.

Adults Automatic Lifejackets
Adults are fitted with jackets that inflate automatically on contact with the water, that also feature a manual inflation toggle.

Children Foam Jackets
Children and toddlers are fitted with foam jackets.

Sailing Jackets
Due to an element of wind-chill in certain conditions on this type of vessel we supply both children and adults with warm waterproof sailing jackets. These are not necessary but they’re available to any customer who requires one.
